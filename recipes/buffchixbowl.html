Big Mac Bowl

Description

Another filling, protein packed dinner that reminds you of hot wings, celery and blue cheese. This dish also provides the enjoyment of hot wings with no guilt, allowing you to reach fitness goals while devouring a tasty meal. Pair this with a nice Diet Coke or Diet Lemonade and you'll be 2 in 2. All you need is some chicken thighs, buffalo sauce / hot sauce, shredded lettuce / cabbage, onions, celery, bleu cheese, greek yogurt, mayo and ranch seasoning.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Shredded Low-Fat American Cheese
  • Diced Onion
  • Diced celery
  • Ranch Seasoning
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Seasoning blend: Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Wash and shred your lettuce and add to a bowl with diced onions and celery.
  2. Combine 1 part mayo, 2 parts greek yogurt and mix in the ranch seasoning packet. Combine the high protein ranch with the chopped lettuce / onion / celery.
  3. Season your chicken thighs with salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder. Cook over medium high heat until meat is cooked all the way through.
  4. Add cooked thighs into the salad and toss with buffalo sauce. Top with lowfat shredded cheese, bleu cheese crumbles, and the rest of our homemade high-protein ranch.
